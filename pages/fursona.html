character vibe

design details

design notes

character energy

  • pink bunny and devil mixed together
  • soft cloud-like tail made from the fluffiest fur
  • tail tip is usually black unless emotions start shifting it
  • found sometimes with a silver saxophone nearby
  • always carrying strawberry gum in pockets
quick facts

little details i really love

  • the note in the eyes changes depending on tone and feeling
  • music does not just sound pretty, it sets the room’s mood
  • looks soft enough to hug, but still has a devil spark
  • the tail tip is surprisingly good at opening Jones cream soda bottles
  • strawberry gum is basically always somewhere in the pockets
tiny lore

why the design feels so personal

  • the bunny side brings the softness and sweetness
  • the devil side gives it that little troublemaker charm
  • the music side makes emotions feel visible instead of hidden
  • the whole thing is meant to feel cute, comforting, and a little dramatic
