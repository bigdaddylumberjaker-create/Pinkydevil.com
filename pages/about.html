who i am

Pinky / PinkyDevil

I'm Pinky, or PinkyDevil. I'm probably way more obsessed with the color pink than I should be. Most of my time is spent making music, working on random projects, or creating things that slowly turn into something real. I try to be someone people feel comfortable around because I know how much a little kindness can matter.

what i do

music + teaching

I teach private saxophone lessons and repair instruments whenever I can. I also help out as a beginner DCI member and occasionally work as a fill-in band director. Music has always been a huge part of my life, and I enjoy helping other people improve just as much as I enjoy playing myself.
